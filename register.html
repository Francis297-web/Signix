About Signix

Deaf-first KSL learning platform built like w3schools for structure, like Khan Academy for progression.

Our Mission

Signix exists to make Kenyan Sign Language fluency accessible to everyone. All content is created with native KSL signers. No audio required. Visual learning first.

Founder: Patience Wambui

Why Signix?

Unlike generic video platforms, Signix uses spaced repetition, fluency paths, and deaf-first design to help you actually achieve fluency — not just watch videos.
