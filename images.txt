INSTRUCTIONS FOR CONTRIBUTORS:
1. All KSL videos must be filmed by native KSL signers
2. Video format: .mp4 or .webm, 720p minimum, 16:9 aspect ratio
3. No audio tracks needed. Add text captions burned-in if possible
4. Thumbnails: .jpg, 640x360px, clear view of handshape
5. Naming: category-signname.mp4  e.g. greetings-hello.mp4

FOLDER STRUCTURE:
/images/ - Thumbnails, logos, UI graphics

CONTRIBUTOR GUIDELINES

1. Film in good lighting, plain background
2. Signer wears solid dark color, no patterns
3. Start/end each video with hands at rest position
4. Hold final handshape for 1 second before ending
5. Include KSL gloss as filename and in captions
6. Get consent from all signers before uploading
